Farkle Rules
* 6 dice
* each roll you have to freeze at least one and xfer it to your hand
* if no dice score on any turn, you farkle and your turn ends
* at the end of your turn you either xfer your hand to your bank or farkle
* 3 farkles erases your pot
* win when your pot = 10,000
* score on all 6 dice and you keep rolling ("hot dice")
* Scoring:
1 = 100
5 = 50
